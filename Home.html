Welcome to the High Performance Computing Laboratory. The research carried out in the laboratory span the areas of high performance, parallel machine learning algorithms, computational finance, language and runtime extensions for multicore and manycore, and vision on GP GPUs and FGPAs. The laboratory employs top graduates as researchers who demonstrate a high research potential. The researchers in the laboratory benefit from state-of-the-art equipment, high quality work and research environments, while recieving research advice from all the lecturers prior with the Dean of the Faculty of Applied Science.
